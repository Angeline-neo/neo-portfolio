Hi, I'm Angeline Neo. (梁佩莹 liang pei ying)

Angeline currently works as a graphic designer at University of Florida, handling all visual design materials while working with the marketing and communications team. Born and raised in Malaysia, currently resides in Florida. Her background is in graphic design, drawing, and UX/UI design. She enjoys translating business and user needs to strong visual communications and intuitive digital products.

Interest

She has a deep passion for fashion, art, and culture. She likes listening to K-pop, trying food from different cultures, and thrift shopping.

Experience

In the past, she has worked for various industries, including healthcare, tourism, manufacturing, non-profits, and higher education. She likes to be challenged and solve problems.

Outside of design

You will find her watching sunsets or drawing. I also enjoy travelling around the world because there is so much to see and learn. I have travelled to 13 countries and 28 U.S. states and more to go!

Dream
